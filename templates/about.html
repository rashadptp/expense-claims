1. Overview

{{ SITE_NAME }} replaces the manual "walk a paper receipt to the finance desk" process across all branches. An employee uploads their receipts; the AI reads and validates them; the claim then flows through a multi-stage approval chain and is finally marked paid and deducted from the branch's monthly budget. Every action is logged for audit.

6. Duplicate detection

A claim is flagged as a possible duplicate (a critical flag, −50) in two ways:

🔑

Exact file match

Every uploaded image is fingerprinted with a SHA-256 hash of its bytes. If the same file appears on another (non-rejected) claim, it's caught — even across different employees.

🧮

Same vendor / amount / date

Even if the photo differs, a claim with the same employee + same amount + same date as an existing one is flagged — catching re-photographed or re-typed duplicates.

7. The AI health score

Each claim gets a score: 100 minus the sum of every flag's penalty (floored at 0). It's a quick signal for approvers — a clean claim scores 100.

80–100
Healthy — no or minor issues.
50–79
Worth a closer look.
0–49
Multiple or serious problems.

The score is advisory; the hard gate is the critical flag — any critical issue forces human review before the claim can advance.

8. Notifications & one-click approval

Email goes out automatically at every step:

  • • Submitted — the employee gets a confirmation; the next approver is notified.
  • • Approved / moved forward — the employee is told, and the next approver gets a request.
  • • Rejected — the employee receives the reviewer's reason.
  • • Paid — the employee gets a final confirmation.

One-click approval from the email

Approver emails include Approve and Reject buttons. They open a secure, signed link — no login required — showing the full claim, where the approver confirms the decision and the claim advances to the next stage. Links are tied to that approver and that stage, and expire after 7 days.

9. Budgets & audit trail

🏢

Branch budgets

Each branch has a monthly petty-cash budget. Paid claims are deducted, and the dashboard shows spend vs. budget so overspend is visible at a glance.

📜

Immutable audit trail

Every status change records who did it, when, the before/after status and any comment — giving accounts a complete, tamper-evident history of each claim.
